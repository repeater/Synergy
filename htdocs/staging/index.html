U.S. Based Call Centers and Customer Service

Combining synergy and innovation to create the next generation of customer contact solutions.

Synergy Solutions is a nationally recognized expert in outsourced call center solutions. Our key focus is on relationships: the relationships that we build in our communities and with our employees translates to the relationships that we strengthen on the phone with your customers. We understand that our Customer Contact Representatives are on the front line with your customers and they have to care about your customers as much as your CEO does. That’s why we have made the commitment to operate all of our Call Centers in the United States and staff our facilities with the best and brightest US based representatives in order to ensure an outstanding customer experience on each and every call.

Our team is comprised of seasoned veterans with expertise in the industries of our clients so we understand your business and what success means to you. Our management team has worked together for two decades demonstrating a commitment to quality, cost-effective and scalable results

We operate from seven US Call Centers and offer inbound, outbound, email, chat, bi-lingual services, and licensed insurance agents.

Our flexible technology allows for seamless integration to client systems as well as completely customized stand-alone solutions. All of our custom solutions are built with security, reliability and redundancy in mind.

We talk to our customers and they tell us that most compelling distinction Synergy offers is the flexibility of our solutions. We have the mature infrastructure shaped by years of experience but have eliminated the bureaucracy and obstacles found at large corporations. We operate in a fast paced dynamic environment where innovation is rewarded, teamwork is evident and delighting our clients is our critical mission.

Synergy Solutions is well positioned for these challenging economic times. With strong year over year growth, solid client retention and no external debt, you can count on us to be here to service your customers. We continuously invest in our growth including capacity expansion, state of the art technology, continuous employee development and recruiting the best and brightest team members.
