ATA-SRO Accreditation

A self-regulatory organization (SRO) is an organization that exercises some degree of regulatory authority over an industry or profession.

In 2006, the American Teleservices Association Self Regulatory Organization (ATA-SRO) originated by a group of ATA members in the wake of Do Not Call Legislation as a means to help regulators identify the great companies who operate in full compliance. The objective of the ATA-SRO also included going beyond existing regulations and encompassing best practices so as to avoid the need for future over-regulation. This initiative was launched with the full and ongoing support of the Federal Trade Commission (FTC) and several members of the United States Congress. Part of this initiative was formulating a set of universal standards reflective of federal and state laws that promote contact center compliance.

Synergy is the seventh company worldwide to achieve this distinguished accreditation. Proud of our ongoing commitment to excellence, SRO accreditation provides our clients formal assurance that Synergy has the policies and procedures to ensure legal compliance and further distinguishes Synergy as a leader in the teleservices industry.
