Join the Synergy Team!

Synergy Solutions is always interested in adding talented people to our team!  If you want to join a dynamic, fast-growing company that has the vision and the resources to provide leading edge customer contact solutions, please apply in person at one of our call center locations. Open positions and shifts vary by location, but include those listed below. If you have questions about a particular position, please contact Lisa Gibbons in Human Resources at (602) 296.1642. Synergy is proud to be an equal opportunity employer.

Customer Contact Representative (CCR)

Inbound and outbound telephone customer service and sales representative. Responsible for professional interaction with customers while achieving service levels, quality and performance goals.

Quality Coach

Monitor Customer Contact Representatives to ensure adherence to quality guidelines. Coach and develop CCR’s to improve quality and performance.

Verification Specialist

Verify sales calls previously handled by a CCR.

Administrative Assistant

Answer telephones and greet guests. Handle all clerical duties such as data entry, copies, filing and faxing.

Supervisor

Responsible for leading a team of CCR’s to meet performance, quality and attendance goals. Monitor, coach, develop and provide leadership to employees.

Trainer

Conduct orientation and training for new and existing employees. Educate employees on all company and client curriculum to ensure their success.

Shift Manager

Manage call center for a specified shift, including multiple supervisor-led teams. Responsible for meeting daily performance, quality and hours goals. Coach, develop and provide leadership to employees.

Center Manager

Responsible for managing all aspects of the call center and for meeting performance, quality and financial goals. Coach, develop and provide leadership to employees.

Synovation = Power of Team + Innovation

At Synergy, our Culture is based on Synovation, which is loosely defined as Synergy (The Power of Team) combined with Innovation. Synovation embodies our organization from the way we operate to the way we provide service to the way we solve problems for our clients. When you harness the power of teamwork and innovation you produce the energy that flows through Synergy and allows us to provide superior service solutions without bureaucracy for our partners.
