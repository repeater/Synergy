Compliance

At Synergy Solutions, we recognize the critical nature of having compliance expertise as part of our mission to offer high quality and ethical teleservices. We are recognized as an established leader in the area of call center compliance and best practices for the teleservices channel. Our commitment is evidenced by:

  • Active Members (Platinum Level) of the American Teleservices Association
  • Company President serves on ATA Board of Directors and founded the Arizona Chapter of the ATA
  • ATA Self Regulatory Organization (SRO) Trustees and membership in the Compliance Officers Forum
  • Expertise in State and Federal Call Center Compliance as well as compliance that impacts our clients including HIPAA, CMS, FCRA, SOX, GLB and others
  • Comprehensive Compliance Training Module for All Employees
  • Employee Compliance Testing at Regular Intervals
  • Attend compliance seminars and subscribe to ATA Regulatory Guide for continuing compliance education
  • Contract with Call Center Industry's Top Compliance Attorneys for guidance
  • All systems certified to support FTC/FCC Compliance Requirements
  • PCI Certified
  • Provide Compliance Consulting to Clients and Small Businesses
  • Multiple Media Appearances Locally and Nationally on Compliance Topics
